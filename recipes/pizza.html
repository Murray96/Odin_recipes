Thin Crust dough Pizza

This thin-crust pizza dough recipe is ready to bake in just 15 minutes with deliciously crisp results.

What You'll Need

  • Yeast: This thin crust pizza dough starts with one teaspoon of active dry yeast.
  • Sugar and water: Activate the yeast in white sugar and lukewarm water.
  • Flour:This crispy thin crust calls for all-purpose flour instead of bread flour.
  • Salt:Salt tightens the gluten structure, which strengthens the dough and makes it more elastic.

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • ¼ teaspoon white sugar
  • ¾ cup lukewarm water
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour, divided
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • Your favourite toppings(e.g pepperoni)

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water that's 110 degrees F (44 degrees C) in a small bowl.
    Let stand until yeast softens and begins to form a creamy foam, about 5 to 8 minutes.
  3. Combine 1 3/4 cups flour and salt in a large bowl. Pour in yeast mixture; mix well until dough comes together.
  4. Transfer dough to a floured surface and knead until smooth, about 2 minutes. Add remaining 1/4 cup flour if dough is too sticky.
  5. Roll dough into a 12-inch circle. Transfer to a greased pizza pan.
  6. Add your favorite toppings
  7. Top crust as desired and bake at 500 degrees F (260 degrees C) until golden, 8 to 10 minutes.
